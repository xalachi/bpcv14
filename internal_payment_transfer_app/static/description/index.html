Internal Payment Transfer in Odoo

Odoo doesn't provide for such options Payment with different account type options with this app helps user to transfer payment internally with many options like account to account, journal to account, and account to journal for voucher in Odoo accounting module.

Account to Account Transfer

User have account to account option to transfer payment.

Journal To Account Journal Transfer

User have journal to account option to transfer payment.

Account to Journal Transfer

User have account to journal option to transfer payment.
